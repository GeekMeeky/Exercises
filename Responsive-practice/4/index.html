Satoru Gojo
五ご条じょう悟さとる

Satoru Gojo is a fictional character.He was first introduced as the mentor of the cursed teenager Yuta Okkotsu at Tokyo Prefectural Jujutsu High SchoolGojo takes the same role but mentors the student Yuji Itadori who suffers a similar Curse, helping him become stronger while protecting other characters in the series.

Light Blue
Tokyo Jujutsu High

Gojo was designed by Gege Akutami to be a formidable yet endearing figure who is passionate about his students. He is voiced by Yūichi Nakamura in Japanese.
